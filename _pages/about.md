As a student pursuing studies in seemingly disparate disciplines, namely the humanities and business administration, I have long been aware of the tendency to treat these fields as fundamentally distinct. This blog serves as a platform to explore their intersection. Its purpose is to challenge the misconception that philosophy and cybersecurity are mutually exclusive or unrelated disciplines, and to examine topics in cybersecurity and artificial intelligence comprehensively through an ethical lens.
